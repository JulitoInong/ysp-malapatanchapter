About our chapter

Sino kami sa Malapatan?

Ang Youth Service Philippines (YSP) – Malapatan Chapter ay isang samahang pinamumunuan ng kabataan na may layuning maging tulay sa pagbabago. Kami ay nakabase sa ating bayan at naniniwala na ang bawat maliit na serbisyo ay may malaking epekto sa ating komunidad.

  • Naglilingkod nang may katapatan para sa mga nangangailangan sa ating barangay.
  • Binubuo ng mga kabataang may malasakit sa kapaligiran at edukasyon.
  • Katuwang ng bawat mamamayan sa pagbuo ng isang mapayapang Malapatan.

Our guiding declaration

Preamble

We, the members of Youth Service Philippines – Malapatan Chapter, with a shared goal of serving our community and helping those in need, hereby establish this Constitution and By-Laws as our guide in carrying out the purpose of our organization.

We recognize the important role of the youth in promoting volunteerism, cooperation, and community service. Through our collective efforts, we aim to provide meaningful programs and activities that will contribute to the welfare and development of the people of Malapatan.

As members of this organization, we shall uphold the values of respect, responsibility, and teamwork. We commit ourselves to work together, support one another, and remain dedicated to the mission of Youth Service Philippines – Malapatan Chapter.

Where we are going

Mission and vision

The principles that keep our service focused, responsible, and meaningful.

Our vision

A transformed society led by capable youth.

To serve as the primary catalyst for a transformed society, led by a corps of altruistic and capable youth dedicated to the principles of integrity, empathy, and selfless service.

Our mission

Turning youth potential into collective action.

The organization exists to harness the collective potential of the youth through service platforms encompassing humanitarian outreach, environmental sustainability, and continuous leadership development.
